Meet Vickram

Hi everyone! I'm Vickram, a tech enthusiast with over a decade of experience in the software industry. My journey started at Amazon, where I began as an intern, followed by seven impactful years at Google, contributing to projects like Google Assistant and TensorFlow. Now, as the CEO of XLaunch, a leading software company I founded, I'm passionate about driving innovation and making coding accessible to everyone. My global travels, from Japan to the U.S., have shaped my perspective, fueling my passion for technology and leadership.

Outside of work, I’m deeply passionate about playing soccer and diving into computer games. Whether it’s a competitive match on the field or an intense gaming session, I find these activities both relaxing and exhilarating. They offer me a balance to my work life and keep my mind sharp. As for food, I’m a huge fan of sushi and a perfectly grilled steak—meals that fuel my energy and keep me going, both on and off the field.
